\section*{Slide 9 --- Analysis and Problems Encountered}

\textbf{Time:} 2 minutes

\textbf{Support Material:}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=0.7cm]
    \item Key findings
    \item Quick Sort behaviour
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Method of Presentation:}

Discussion of important observations.

\textbf{What I Say:}

One interesting result was that
Insertion Sort became much faster
when the input was almost sorted.

Another important observation
was the behaviour of Quick Sort
on structured inputs.

At first, Quick Sort caused a stack overflow because the pivot selection produced very deep recursion. After changing the implementation to a median-of-three pivot, the algorithm became much more stable.

This shows that practical implementations
can behave differently from theoretical expectations.
